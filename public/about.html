About Dashiga

"Sharing information that someone like you would be looking for."

This was the motive that led to making Dashiga. People are all unique, yet a number of them share characteristics, characteristics that differentiate their group from others. The passion for designing, similar area of residence, age, nationality, whatever it is there are things that only someone in in the group you're part of would be interested in. Dashiga is a platform where people can make communities, where the means to maintaining the necessary size, efficiency in distributing information is provided. Providing means to sharing what you know or think in diverse forms is another goal that Dashiga is focused towards. It is currently in its beta testing phase, where the first community called "Foreigners in Korea" has been created to aid foreigners find their way and share their perspectives about Korea, from their point of view. A lot of the functions that are necessary, and will provide Dashiga with its unique identity, are still to come. The reason it has been released so early in its stage of development is to get enough user feedback to provide users with a service more relevant to their needs.
